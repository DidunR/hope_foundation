Hope Groups

Project history

Hope Groups is a project, launched after the beginning of the war in Ukraine on 24.02.22 by World Without Orphans, Children's Mission, Viva Together for Children, when millions of people were forcibly displaced and desperately needed psychological support and stress relief.

Concept

The idea was to gather small groups of women and together with the facilitator study a specially developed guide. This information allows us to remind ourselves of our emotional health, which is the last thing we care about, and on which our physical condition directly depends.

Topics for discussion

- how to understand and analyze your condition; - tools for calming and stabilization; - how to maintain positive and safe relationships in the family; - advice on how to talk to children; - life resilience and how to develop it in oneself.

Results and effectiveness

These meetings eventually build strong relations among the group members, provide unconditional support and care, give a strong sense of belonging and start healing processes in all spheres of life. Hope groups - is a powerful instrument for strengthening and educating a community, which was researched and proven by Oxford University.

Healing for families

Part of the guide is dedicated to saving and strengthening families and supporting children. It is full of advice on how to create a safe and sincere circle among the family members, behave a caring attitude towards others, and help heal trauma and stress consequences.

Self support tools

After going through 12 sessions of Hope Groups, people acquire skills on how to analyze their psychological condition and steps to overcome and improve the situation. The basic techniques and practices, given in the guide, are teaching how to indicate that you need help and how to find help, which is essential for parents, who can teach their children to become resilient wholesome personalities.
